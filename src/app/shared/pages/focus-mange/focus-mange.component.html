Routing Focus (Page to page navigation)

When navigating between pages in Angular, there is no management focus.

This makes it very difficult for keyboard users and assistive technology users to be able to understand where they are or even if they moved pages.

  1. Move focus to first heading
  2. Move focus to main body of page
  3. Move focus to skip link
